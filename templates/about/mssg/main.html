Mission

One of the founding values of the MSG is the belief that a comprehensive approach needs to be taken when furthering human activity in space. For this reason the MSG is a multi-disciplinary group that is comprised of Technical Research, CSDC Design Teams, Education, and Conferences. With these different facets, the MSG acts as a key contributor in the Canadian space sector.
